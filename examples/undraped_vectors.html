--- title: Undraped vectors shortdesc: Display GeoJSON files as meshes and symbols. longdesc: The FeatureCollection entity can display simple features as meshes, that do not require a map to display. The benefits are a reduced memory usage and lower latency when updating the styles. The entity supports fully dynamic fill, stroke and point styles. Whenever the style of a feature changes, call updateStyles() with the updated object(s). dependencies: ['colormap'] tags: [mesh, style, vectors] ---
