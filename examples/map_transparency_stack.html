--- title: Stacking transparent maps shortdesc: Illustrates how transparency in maps work. longdesc: Transparency in maps (and any other 3D object) is a tricky problem. Due to limitations in how 3D renderers work, it is not generally possible to correctly display overlapping transparent objects. For example, set the opacity of at least 2 maps to less than 100%, rotate the camera around, and observe various rendering issues, such as missing map tiles. attribution: © IGN, © iTowns ---
